Market Update

What the San Antonio Market Is Actually Telling Us Right Now

The honest answer is that timing the market perfectly is a losing game. The people who win in real estate are the ones who buy when it makes sense for their situation, not when they think they've found the perfect moment. That said, there are real signals worth paying attention to right now.

San Antonio is sitting at about 4.9 months of inventory. That's closer to a balanced market than the extreme seller's market we saw a few years ago. Buyers have more options and more negotiating room than they've had in years. Sellers who price correctly are still moving quickly. The ones who don't are sitting.

Rates are still the elephant in the room for a lot of people. But here's what I tell buyers who are waiting for rates to drop: if rates go down significantly, every buyer who's been sitting on the sidelines jumps into the market at the same time. Prices go up. The affordability gain you were waiting for disappears. The buyers who move in a higher rate environment often get better deals because there's less competition.

The right time to buy is when you're financially ready, when the right property is available, and when your life is ready for it. The market is a factor but it's rarely the deciding one. What's your situation right now?
