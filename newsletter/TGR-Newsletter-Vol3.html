Military Home Buying

Why San Antonio Is One of the Best Markets in the Country for Military Families

San Antonio has the second-largest military presence in the United States. That shapes everything here. Lenders know VA loans inside and out, sellers understand PCS timelines, and neighborhoods around JBSA have been absorbing military buyers for decades. You're not educating anyone when you show up with orders. They already know how this works.

The market is also genuinely affordable by major city standards. A solid three-bedroom home with a yard, the kind where you can actually put down roots and let the kids roam, is still attainable on BAH in many parts of the city. That's increasingly rare in military markets like San Diego, D.C., or Colorado Springs.

And there's a strong sense of community here that military families tend to appreciate quickly. Neighborhoods near the bases are full of people who understand deployments, late-night duty calls, and the particular kind of resilience it takes to move every two or three years. San Antonio tends to feel like home faster than most duty stations.

If you're PCS-ing to JBSA and trying to sort out VA loans, BAH, neighborhoods, and timelines all at once, I put together a complete guide for exactly that situation. Everything you need in one place before you ever get on a call with anyone.
