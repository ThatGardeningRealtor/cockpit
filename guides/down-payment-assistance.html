🌱 Down Payment Assistance
Programs that help San Antonio buyers get into a home
with little or no money down.
Did you know?

San Antonio and Bexar County have programs that can give you up to $30,000 toward buying a home — and some don't need to be repaid.

How to Apply for Assistance

When contacting any of these programs, have the following ready:

  • Proof of income (pay stubs, award letters)
  • Government-issued ID
  • Rental agreement or mortgage statement
  • Any notices from landlord or lender

Apply early — funds are limited and demand is high.
